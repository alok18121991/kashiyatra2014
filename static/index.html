The Painting Competition

" Through my art I want to bring my tinted visions to life, I want to convert the commotion of my mind into colorful madness on paper..."

Using simple ideas the participants are required to portray a particular theme with the help of given articles over a piece of paper. Unconventionally, the participants will not be provided any pencils or erasers to make the painting. However, they can use cotton, threads, thumbs, toothbrushes, paint brushes (optional), etc. to make it.

Rules:

  • Team (2 members) as well as individual event.
  • Sheets, paints, cotton, threads, toothbrushes and a paintbrush (optional) will be provided.
  • Theme will be announced on the spot.
  • On Spot Registration allowed.
  • Maximum time- 2 hours .

Marks Distribution:

  • Relevance to the theme
  • Use of materials provided
  • Creativity

The glass painting competition

"The simple transparent layers would come together to reflect a complete story..."

Glass painting usually implies to a painting on the surface of a transparent sheet/glass. In this event transparent sheet(s) will be provided to the participants. They are required to paint the sheets and assemble them in any decorative fashion that would reflect the theme. The colours and brushes along with other materials will be provided on spot.

Rules:

  • Max memebers-3
  • Time limit-3 hours
  • Theme will be given on spot
  • Transparent sheets/glass, acrylic paints, brushes, cotton etc will be given on spot.
  • On spot registration allowed

The Still-life sketching Competition

"When I paint a still life , I want it to be anything but still. I want it to shimmer with light, I want it to rustle with movement..."

Still life is all about studying a set of still objects and sketching it using proper sense of proportions, light and shade.

Rules:

  • It is an individual event.
  • Pencils (HB, 4B and 8B), erasers, cutters, charcoal, cotton and sheets will be provided.
  • A set of objects will be placed on spot.
  • Spot Registration allowed.
  • Maximum time- 2 hours

The Paper Costume Design Competition

"Be your fantasies and wear your creations as you express yourself while playing with the paper"

Designing a costume might seem to be a very exquisite work for many of you. This event lets you do the same but in a different fashion i.e. using papers. Gaang Baazi

Back

"Let The Gang Wars Begin!!"
The Flagship Dance Event of KASHIYATRA, GAANG-BAAZI features some of the most explosive dance talent from across the country, competing in arguably the most intense event at KASHIYATRA. Come, knit hands, and beat the ground, in the fantastic round. The deafening roar of a mass of frenzied spectators will surely pump your adrenalin to the extreme—so all you need to do is grace the stage and BEAT THE REST TO BE THE BEST!!

Rules & guidelines:


  • Team size:
    • Elimination round and finals: Min 5 members and max 22 members are allowed in a team. A Max. of 15 members is allowed on stage at a time.

  • Elimination round:
    • Teams will have to perform to a suitably edited part of the music from their main performance
    • The music which is used in the elimination round has to be a part of the first round of the finals.
    • Time limit: 3 mins

  • Final Round:
    • 8 teams will qualify for the finals
    • Teams present their prepared performances
    • Time limit: 12 mins.
    • There is no constraint on the Dance Form.
    • Costumes and props will be included in the judging criteria in this round.

  • Judging criteria:
    Synchronization, choreography, expressions, energy level, stage utilization, costumes and props, overall impact, innovation and presentation.
    (Note: For Elimination round costumes and props won’t be included in the judging criteria, otherwise the judging criteria of elimination round are same as that of finals.)

This is it – The Battle to decide who’s the One above ‘em all. We provide you the best platform to showcase your talent as a dancer and as a performer. The thumping applause, the beats and an electrifying atmosphere await you...

Rules & guidelines:

  • Judging criteria:
    • Round 1: Choreography, expressions, energy level, stage utilization, costumes and props, innovation, overall impact.
      (Note: For Elimination round costumes and props won't be included in the judging criteria, otherwise the judging criteria of elimination round are same as of round 1 of finals.)

"Ek Main aur Ek Tu"
With its ever increasing popularity and also made famous by many reality shows, we would like to take this event to new heights. Since its debut, this event has given many awe-inspiring moments and taken things to the next level. It is a duet dance competition wherein registrations will be in couples (two boys or two girls or a boy and a girl- any combination is welcome).

Rules & guidelines:


  • It is a pair dance event. Any two individuals can pair up (irrespective of their gender) to participate in this event.

  • Elimination round:
    • Time limit: Max 2 mins.
    • The costumes and props won’t be a part of the judging criteria in this round.
    • The participants have to perform to a part of the final dance


  • Finals:
  • The pairs that qualify the Elimination round will perform in the Finals that consist of 2 rounds.

  • Round 1:
    • The teams present their prepared performances
    • There is NO RESTRICTION on Dance Forms/Styles
    • Time limit: Max 5 mins.

  • Round 2:
    • As soon as the results of the elimination round are announced, each of the selected pairs will be given a theme which has to be depicted in their performance in this round.
    • The music is of participant's choice
    • Time limit: Min. 2 mins and Max 5 mins.



  • Judging criteria:
    • Round 1: Synchronization, choreography, expressions, energy level, costumes and props, innovation, presentation and overall impact.
      (Note: For Elimination round costumes and props won't be included in the judging criteria, otherwise the judging criteria are same as of round 1 of finals.)

    • Round 2: : Effective presentation and depiction of the theme, innovation, choreography, synchronization, usage of props, costumes, energy level, overall impact.

RANGMANCH ( Stage Play Competition)

Rules & guidelines:


  • 4 chairs and 2 tables will be provided.
  • Any other production material has to be brought by the teams themselves.
  • TIME LIMIT: 30 - 40 minutes (including the stage set up and clearance time)
  • Performance should be in Hindi or English or both. No other languages will be allowed.
  • Stage Play comprises of multiple scenes and may have blackouts.
  • Backstage voiceovers and narrations are allowed during the performance.
  • Pre-recorded music is allowed and should be brought in a CD/DVD or a pen drive in .mp3 format only.
  • Obscenity (at the discretion of the judges) of any kind is not allowed and will lead to immediate disqualification.
  • Time Limit should be strictly followed. Participants will be penalized by judges for not doing so.
  • Participants will be provided a light control box, from which the lighting on the stage can be controlled.
  • The performance can be an original or an adaptation. There are no constraints regarding that.
  • There is no restriction on team size.
  • Any kind of fluid or flame is not allowed on stage.
  • The decision of the judges will be Final and binding.


  • Judging criteria:
    Screenplay, Direction, Script, Acting and Overall Impact.

Oldest Rock Band Competition in India - Annual Rock Band Competition of IIT (BHU), will make you mosh during Kashiyatra 2014.


Unique in its being among the few multilingual rock fests of the country, Crosswindz transcends boundaries of language, and region; here, only rock prevails. Bigger and better than ever before, Crosswindz this year will get your brains quaking and your blood pumping. Breaking through all limits, and crossing all lines of sanity with screaming guitars, roaring drums and wailing vocals shall be bands from all parts of the country. In Kashiyatra 2014, IIT (BHU) will rock.

Description
Since time immemorial, IIT (BHU) has been home to rock in India. With epic bands like 'Beefcake' and 'Mute' passing from these hallowed portals, headbanging is part of our curriculum. Since the first riff was heard, music lovers have descended once a year to this mecca of music at a celebration of the free human spirit we like to call Crosswindz, the rock band event of Kashiyatra the annual cultural festival of IIT-BHU. Multitudes of phenomenal bands have set the stage ablaze at Crosswindz. Parikrama, Skinny Alley, Medesis have all been here, performing, winning, rocking. \m/


Rules:

  • Time per band : 25 mins including soundcheck.
  • Atleast one Original Composition is compulsory.

Kashiyatra, the annual cultural festival of IIT (BHU) Varanasi is, as the name suggests, a journey encompassing tradition, culture and heritage with the modern outlook of the 21st century. Kashiyatra is four days of celebration of the grandeur of Kashi with the spirit of youth. It is four days of unhindered fun and entertainment, a time to let loose, absorb the vibrance and revel in the roots of our culture.

Kashiyatra is also one of the largest cultural festivals of North India, a platform for hundreds of competitors from all parts of India to showcase their talents to an audience in the tens of thousands in a variety of forms ranging from music and dance to literature, theatre and fine arts. The amalgamation of talent, of songs and dance, artists and performers makes Varanasi come alive on February nights.

Kashiyatra will boast of many firsts, an international MUN, treasure hunts across Varanasi, and exciting new events.

Antarnaad

Final night of Kashiyatra sees famous artists from the Indian Film Industry enthrall the thousands in audience with collection of their songs and performances. Lucky Ali, Underground Authority, Javed Ali, Indian Ocean, and many others. With Antarnaad, KY always leaves with a blast.
